05 QR Lock-In Most intuitive

The literal continuation of the physical moment the juror just lived through. A stylized QR renders pixel-by-pixel, the three finder brackets snap into place, a laser scan sweeps top-to-bottom, and the whole surface resolves into a green success disc with an outward ring-burst. Instantly legible — "yes, the scan worked, you're in."

Motion~2.3s choreography: brackets pop → grid fills → laser sweep → finders snap → check disc + ring burst. Content cascades in after the burst.
06 Vault Unlock Most premium

Cryptographic ceremony. A shield stroke draws itself, fills with soft blue, then an emerald checkmark strokes in on top. Three concentric energy rings expand outward at staggered offsets. Reads as "session key verified" — rigor without being clinical. Pairs naturally with the editorial type below.

MotionShield path draws 0→1s, fill fades in 1.6s, check draws 1.6–2s, rings pulse 1.5–3s. Content reveals after 1.6s.
07 Constellation Verify Distinctive

Network-grade identity. A center node radiates radar pings, outer constellation nodes fade in from the edges, then hairline connection lines stroke-draw between them. Ends on "All nodes online." The metaphor fits the multi-tenant architecture cleanly — this is the variant to use if VERA's story becomes "the network for academic juries."

MotionPings 0–1.5s, nodes fade in 0.4–1.2s staggered, lines stroke-draw 1.4–2.1s. Content reveals after 2.4s.
08 Cipher Decrypt Most memorable

Text decrypts from scrambled monospace characters into "TED UNIVERSITY" letter-by-letter, then the editorial serif headline materializes out of blur above it. Conveys the invisible handshake (entry-token verify, tenant resolution) as a visible spectacle without feeling theatrical. The most "wow first-impression" option.

MotionCursor blink + cipher scramble 0–0.7s, letter-by-letter reveal 0–1.2s, serif hero blur-in 2–3s, CTA cascade after.
Which one to ship → Concept 05 (QR Lock-In) is the strongest default — it closes the loop on the physical QR scan the juror just performed, and the success burst makes the moment feel earned rather than decorative. Concept 06 (Vault Unlock) is the premium fallback if the QR pixel grid feels too busy against the rest of the app; the shield-draw carries the same trust signal with more restraint. Concept 08 (Cipher Decrypt) is the one to use in demo / marketing screenshots — it photographs beautifully and reads as distinctive tech craft. Concept 07 (Constellation) is a long-bet option — save it for the moment you want to reframe VERA as a platform story rather than a tool story.
